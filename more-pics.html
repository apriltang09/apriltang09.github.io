Pics of Betsie

Betsie loves to play with boxes. She digs her head in and sniffs around, hoping to find some new friends to play. She sometimes like to get in a box and twirl around a few times before settling in.

Betsie is an observer by nature. She loves to stare at things, especially small bugs and birds. She would speak to the flies in a language only she knows.

What a lazy cat! Betsie likes to take long naps in sunny, bright areas so that she can watch the small creatures outside and wait for me to get home.
