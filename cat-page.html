Betsie the Cat

Playtime

Betsie is her name and playing is her game! Betsie is a fiesty tabby with an independent streak and a mind of her own. She likes to unroll yarn, pitter pattering around the living room floor. She also likes to speak to flies and try to swat them with her tenny tiny paws. Although she has yet to successfully capture those little buggers, that doesn't stop her from trying!

Naptime

Besides playing, Betsie's second favorite activity is napping. On top of her cat house, on top of the refrigerator. Basically, anywhere high up so that she can reign over the house and make sure everyone is in line! She also likes sunny spots so on the window ceil is where you'll find her on a hot summer's day.

Daily Schedule

  1. Open eyes.
  2. Meow at my master for food.
  3. Lay down after the heavy meal.
  4. Take a long nap.
  5. Rinse and Repeat.

P.S.

Betsie likes treats, lots and lots. She will eat your food when you're not looking but she will make sure to leave some crumbs behind so you know she was there!
